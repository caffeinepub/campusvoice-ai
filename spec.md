# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Populate the CampusVoice backend with the full list of academic departments and ensure all dropdowns in the frontend display the complete list.

**Planned changes:**
- Replace the backend department seed data with all 30+ academic departments grouped by faculty (Computer Science, Life Sciences, Physical Sciences, Commerce & Management, Arts & Applied Sciences), each with UG/PG level indicated in the description
- Ensure the department dropdown in the complaint submission form displays all seeded departments, scrollable without truncation
- Ensure the department dropdown in the profile setup modal displays all seeded departments, scrollable without truncation

**User-visible outcome:** Students and admins see the full list of academic departments in all department dropdowns, and the admin Department Management page shows all seeded entries.
